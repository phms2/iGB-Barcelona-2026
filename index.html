Understanding the Packages

Each location can be secured with one of three package types, depending on your needs for convenience versus customization.

Branded Shell

The most convenient, all-inclusive option. The stand is built and furnished for you, including branded walls, TV, power, and carpet. Ideal for minimizing logistical overhead.

Example (9sqm Betfred Stand)

Space Only

This option provides maximum branding flexibility. We rent the floor space and hire our own contractor to design and build a custom stand. This is best for a unique presence but requires more project management.

Show Ready

A middle ground where you choose from pre-designed, modern stand styles. This reduces design effort but does not include furniture, which must be rented separately.

Availability Note:

This option is only confirmed to work for the larger 19.5sqm (81-J74) position.
